Calgary's Finest Bars

A Calgary Friday Night Ritual

When we first moved to Calgary, we found ourselves drawn to the vibrant downtown scene, eager to carve out our own tradition. What started as an occasional outing soon became a cherished ritual—one that gave us an excuse to dress up, unwind, and savor the evening with friends. It’s become more than just a night out; it’s a celebration of good drinks, great company, and the city we now call home.

Our ritual usually kicks off at Betty Lou's Library Speakeasy, a hidden gem with a 1920’s-era theme that feels like stepping back in time. With its dim lighting, vintage décor, and expertly crafted cocktails, it’s the perfect place to set the mood for the evening. We usually get a reservation from 6:00pm to 8:00pm (they do have a two-hour maximum, after all), which is just enough time to enjoy a couple of their signature drinks and soak in the intimate ambiance.

After that, we head to Ricardo’s Hideaway, a tropics-inspired rum bar that transports us straight to an island oasis. The menu here features genuine renditions of classic rum and mezcal cocktails, each one thoughtfully crafted and bursting with flavor. If you’re a fan of daiquiris, you’re in for a treat—their version is hands-down the best!

To cap it off, we make our way to Añejo, a lively Mexican restaurant and bar specializing in tequila and offering an array of signature margaritas. From the moment we walk in, we’re greeted by the vibrant atmosphere and delicious smells of fresh tacos. Their "Halfy-hour," where tacos and margaritas are half off after 9:00pm, is the perfect way to end the night on a high note.

This little tradition has become our favorite way to slow down the week, and while it’s evolved over time, it remains a simple joy we look forward to each month. Whether you’re new to Calgary or a long-time local, it’s a ritual that’s sure to make you fall in love with the city’s nightlife just as we did.

Betty Lou's Library

Step into a world where the charm of 1920’s Parisian France meets the mysterious allure of Prohibition-era North America. Betty Lou's Library is Calgary's hidden speakeasy, offering a captivating atmosphere steeped in vintage elegance. The dress code is upscale—think cocktail attire with a touch of flair—perfect for those looking to embrace the glamour of the past while enjoying a night out in the present.

The menu is a tribute to the classics, featuring rotating specialty drinks that keep things fresh while paying homage to timeless cocktail culture. If you're a fan of Old Fashioneds, you're in for a treat—Betty Lou's serves an entire menu dedicated to these iconic drinks, each one named after Ernest Hemingway novels, offering a literary twist to your experience. The cocktail menu is equally enchanting, with each drink named after a famous 1920’s woman, like Esther Jones, Zelda Fitzgerald, and Blanche Calloway, making each sip feel like a step back in time.

And for those who like to nibble while they sip, Betty Lou’s also offers beautifully curated charcuterie boards, the perfect accompaniment to their expertly crafted cocktails. Whether you're a history buff, a cocktail aficionado, or just looking for a unique spot to enjoy a drink, Betty Lou’s Library offers an unforgettable experience steeped in charm and history.

Ricardo's Hideaway

Don’t let the "Tiki bar" label fool you, Ricardo's Hideaway is far from the sugary, gimmicky spots you might find in tourist traps. This is a sophisticated rum bar that pays genuine homage to the classic cocktails of the Caribbean and beyond. With a modern, mid-century flair in the décor, Ricardo’s blends an authentic island vibe with a stylish, contemporary twist. The space is inviting yet refined, with tropical touches that feel like a sophisticated nod to a bygone era of cocktail culture.

What truly sets Ricardo’s apart is their unwavering commitment to quality. Each drink is a carefully crafted masterpiece, made with balanced flavors that honor the traditions of tiki while avoiding the pitfalls of overly sweet, watered-down concoctions. As any seasoned cocktail enthusiast will tell you, you can judge the skill of a bartender by their ability to make a classic drink, and at Ricardo's, their daiquiri is the gold standard. Clean, crisp, and perfectly balanced, it’s arguably the best coconut daiquiri you’ll ever taste—a smooth, refreshing drink that highlights the skill and care that goes into each cocktail.

While rum is the star of the show, Ricardo's also offers a curated selection of gin and mezcal tequila for those who like to mix things up. But it’s the rum that steals the spotlight, with cocktails that evoke the spirit of the islands without resorting to the overly sweet, gimmicky flavors often associated with tiki drinks.

At Ricardo’s, it’s all about authenticity, craftsmanship, and the art of the perfect pour. Whether you’re a rum aficionado or just looking for a stylish spot to enjoy a well-made drink, Ricardo’s Hideaway delivers an unforgettable experience with every sip.

Añejo

When it comes to a fun, vibrant spot to end the night, Añejo is our go-to destination—especially on a Friday night when we’re looking to hang out with friends or colleagues. With its colorful decor, lively atmosphere, and passionate staff, Añejo creates the perfect environment to unwind, enjoy great food, and sip on delicious drinks.

The menu here is a celebration of Mexican flavors, and the fresh guacamole is the ideal way to kick off your meal. It’s always made to order, with ripe avocados and just the right blend of spices, making it a perfect pairing with their standout margaritas. Speaking of margaritas, Añejo’s tequila selection is nothing short of impressive, boasting an extensive range of premium tequilas that elevate each cocktail to new heights. Their margaritas—especially during the Halfy-hour—are a must-try, and at $6.00, they’re an absolute steal.

If you're craving something to eat, the tacos are a perfect choice. They’re loaded with fresh, bold flavors, and at just $3.00 each during Halfy-hour, they make dining here an incredibly cost-effective way to enjoy a night out. Whether you’re a fan of classic taco fillings or something with a twist, Añejo delivers on every front.

Halfy-hour runs after 9:00pm, but if you're hoping to snag a spot, especially on a busy Friday or weekend, a reservation is essential. Añejo fills up fast, and for good reason—the combination of incredible food, unbeatable drink specials, and a dynamic atmosphere makes it one of Calgary’s hottest spots.

If you’re in the mood for a fun night of great drinks, fresh tacos, and the kind of ambiance that’s perfect for socializing, Añejo never disappoints.
